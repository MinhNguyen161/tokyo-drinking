History

Izakaya Ten’s journey began on February 2nd 2010, in the heart of the place which is known as Little Japanese Town in Saigon now – Thai Van Lung area. Welcoming and relaxed, active and hectic, Izakaya Ten offers an unparalleled dining experience that showcases traditional Japanese dishes. Taste of Tokyo. Drink in Tokyo style. Izakaya Ten is really a best place to team up and hang out with your friend for whole night
